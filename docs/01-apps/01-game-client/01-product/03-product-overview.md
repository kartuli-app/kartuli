# Product Overview

## Product definition

`kartuli.app` is a Georgian language learning product where students practice through short lessons and simple games.

## Core promise

When a student has a few free minutes, the product should help them feel:

**"I practiced some Georgian."**

## Product principles

- Product structure comes before visual flourish.
- Practice sessions should stay short and usable.
- Learning is lesson-based and game-backed.
- Study is available as review, but it does not gate Play.
- The product is mobile-first, with desktop support extending the same structure.

## Product shape

The product is built around a simple rhythm:

- choose what to practice
- optionally review it
- play through a generated practice session
- continue with another resource

That rhythm should stay understandable even as more content and features are added.

## Brand, mascot, and tone

- Brand name: `kartuli.app`
- The primary mascot is a Georgian dog illustration.
- The mascot is the main recurring personality layer across the product.
- The voice stays clear, warm, encouraging, and lightly playful.
- Humor should stay small, occasional, and charming rather than noisy or distracting.
- Low-content and recovery states should use the mascot and brand to avoid feeling empty or generic.
- Copy should stay easy to scan and should not carry all of the personality by itself.
